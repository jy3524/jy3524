Long Live the Web
Openness and Universality are important features of the Web.
However, we do not have all the freedom that we are supposed to have on the internet.
There are so many regulations by government and companies.
Internet should be controlled by people, not dominated by centralized organizations.
The Web belongs to us and is vital to democracy.

As We May Think
As Bush predicted, we are surrounded by information due to the advancement of technology.
We can access to a large amount of information via internet.
However, people might get overwhelmed if there is too much information.
Deciding what information to gather is challenging.
Because there are a large amount of inaccurate/biased information.
It is hard to identify which content is accurate.
