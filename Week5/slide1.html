Experiencing “into time. com”

Previous Next

When you first access the website, you are given a full browser window of a pulsing gradient. If you click on the screen, the gradients split into two, and so on.

This piece is an example of web-based abstract art that relies on the viewer’s participation in order for the piece to be made.

Each individual viewer will have their own unique composition when visiting the website since there is an endless number of potential images that can be created with the gradients’ infinite divisions and constantly changing colors.
