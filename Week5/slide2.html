Rozendaal created it in 2010 using Flash before later moving to JavaScript.
The piece is among over 100 of Rozendaal’s single-purpose websites, which usually follow a similar theme of interactive experiences that fill up the entire browser window.
The work is part of the Neen movement, the first art movement of the 21st century comprised of visual artists who used computers/websites to create interesting pieces of art.
